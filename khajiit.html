Khajiit subspecies

For obvious reasons all quadrupeds have zero skills in those that require opposable thumbs- Archery, Handcraft, One Handed, Two Handed, Smith. They can learn some alchemy from eating ingredients. To make up for the lack of weapons, quads have higher Unarmed skill, as well as a bite attack. It is also worth noting that they do wear clothing, although obviously it requires some help to put on.

Ohmes resemble Bosmer or even Altmer (aside from the tail!) and so can disguise themselves more easily, unless they have unique tattoos. Being less bestial, they have more charisma to man and mer, but are shorter than average (about 5 feet) and thus less strong. An Ohmes character's back story is inclined to include diplomacy amongst men and mer, whether politics or espionage.

Dagi are small (3 to 4 feet) and light, live in the jungle treetops, and are magically inclined. A Dagi character is suited best to stealth magic and unconventional tactics. Due to the heat and humidity of their home, natives of Tengmar Forest are habitually scantily dressed, which scandalizes conservative desert dwellers.

Suthay are the common cat-men seen in Morrowind, and thus have base stats. They are inclined to be rogues, rangers, and other light to medium styles.

Cathay are similar to Suthay, only larger(6-7 feet) and thus more inclined to mixing heavier tactics in with guerrilla styles.

Alfiq appear to be house cats. Apparently they cannot speak normally, but are reputed to have magical abilities and be used as spies and saboteurs.

Pahmar are jaguar sized, swift and vicious. They are best suited to stealth kills, especially in forests where they can drag the victims into the trees.

Senche are tiger sized, 800 pounds when full grown, and serve as tanks and intelligent battlemounts. They are most effective against lightly armed and armored opponents who are easy to maul and trample while the Senche riders shoot and spear.

For further explanation of the various kinds of Khajiit, see this page for details.

Mage

  • Alteration manipulates the laws of nature and physical objects.
  • Conjuration summons creatures and equipment from other planes.
  • Destruction damages attributes.
  • Enchantment binds the energy of living beings to objects.
  • Illusion alters perception.
  • Restoration restores attributes.

Warrior

  • Archery: bows, crossbows, darts, blowguns
  • Block: use of shields
  • Heavy Armor: obvious
  • One Handed: daggers, axes, and other smaller weapons
  • Two Handed: broadswords, warhammers, spears, etc.
  • Unarmed: the use of fists and feet

Rogue

  • Acrobatics: jumping, running, climbing
  • Disguise: the ability to maintain a false identity
  • Light Armor: obvious
  • Fraud: blackmail, forgery, embezzlement, insider trading
  • Security: lockpicking, escaping
  • Sneak: stealth, sleight of hand, pickpocket

Peasant

  • Alchemy
  • Speech: barter, persuade
  • Linguistics: how well you learn new languages
  • Handcraft: working in mundane materials like wood, clay, cloth
  • Perform: singing, dancing, erotic
  • Smith: working with metals
