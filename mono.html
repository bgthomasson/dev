Source Code Pro

"There lived here then, away at the North, a beautiful princess, who was also a powerful sorceress. All her magic was used to help the people, and she was never known to hurt anyone who was good."

Zero is 0 and capital "o" is O. 0O0O0O. Numeral one is 1 and lowercase L is l. 1l1l1l1l. ZERO. Z3r0. lollypop. 1011yp0p. #010101.

Similar to Fira Mono and DejaVu Sans Mono, they all use the the hooked l and the dotted 0. Honestly I like the l best but prefer the slashed zero *shrugs*.
